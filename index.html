Our Professional Services

We provide comprehensive repair and rewinding solutions for all types of industrial and commercial electrical equipment with precision and expertise.

Motor Repair & Rewinding

Complete repair and rewinding services for all types of electric motors, single-phase and three-phase.

  • Burnt winding replacement
  • Insulation testing
  • Bearing replacement
  • Balancing and alignment
  • Energy efficiency improvement

Brake Coil Repair

Repair and rewind electromagnetic brake coils used in cranes, hoists, and elevators.

  • Coil rewinding
  • Resistance checking
  • Insulation & varnish treatment
  • Replacement of worn parts

Transformer Repair

Servicing and rewinding of small and medium-capacity transformers up to 11kV.

  • Oil filtration & testing
  • Core and coil rewinding
  • Bushing & terminal replacement
  • Insulation resistance check
  • Vacuum drying process

AC/DC Motor Repair

Expert repair services for both AC and DC motors used in drives, fans, and conveyors.

  • Armature rewinding
  • Commutator machining (DC motors)
  • Rotor balancing
  • Stator & field coil repairs
  • Brush and brush holder maintenance

Servo Motor Repair

Precise diagnostics and repair of servo motors from various brands for CNC and automation.

  • Encoder replacement
  • Feedback alignment
  • Shaft balancing
  • Winding insulation test
  • Dynamic load testing

Compressor Motor Repair

Repair services for electric motors used in air compressors, refrigeration, and HVAC systems.

  • Overheating problem diagnosis
  • Motor bearing & winding check
  • Electrical load balancing
  • On-site troubleshooting & servicing

Atlas Compressor Motor Repair

Complete maintenance and repair solutions for Atlas Copco compressor motors, ensuring high performance, efficiency, and extended equipment life.

  • Motor rewinding and refurbishment
  • Fault diagnosis and troubleshooting
  • Rotor/stator repairs
  • Vibration analysis and balancing
  • Genuine parts replacement

Electro Magnetic Brake Coil Repair

Professional repair and rewinding services for electro magnetic brake coils used in various industrial applications, with precision and reliability.

  • Coil rewinding and replacement
  • Insulation and continuity testing
  • Armature and brake drum servicing
  • Custom coil manufacturing
  • Thermal protection and efficiency upgrades
